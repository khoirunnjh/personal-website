OLLIE



One of my favorite moments while working at 7 Eleven was when I met my beloved cat, Ollie and her other sisters. I found her in a box beside the door entrance during my lunch break. At first, I was hesitant to take her back home but after I ask for my parents permission and they approved it, I brought her back home after I ended my shift.

Before Ollie, I had another cat named "Tompok" but sadly he died of an illness. Ever since the lost of Tompok, I have always wanted a new cat then Ollie came to my life. Perhaps it's a coincidence because Ollie had a black and white spots similar to Tompok. She was the smallest but she was as energetic as her sisters. There was never a dull moment when spending time with them.



I still remember how hard it was when I had to go to Kedah because Ollie and her sisters would be left alone but thankfully I have my parents and siblings to take care for her. Sadly, while I was in Kedah, both of her sisters had run away. However, I am still thankful that I am able to go home and see Ollie.

As day passed by, Ollie grewed up and gave birth to many kittens. However, many of them never stays meaning some will passed away while they were still kittens and some will go missing. For instance, Recently Ollie just gave birth to 5 kittens but now there's only 3 kittens left. One went missing and another one died due to a car accident. It was a very hard time for me but it made me want to appreciate Ollie and the rest of my cats while they are still here. More pictures of my ollie and other cats here.
