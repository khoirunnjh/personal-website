ABOUT ME


Hello! My name is Khoirun Najihah binti Abdul Haris. You can call me Khoirun or Najihah or any nickname that has a good meaning to it. Soon I will be turning 20 years old on 7 December 2021, although according to the Islamic calendar I'm already 20 because my birthday was on 21 Ramadhan which has already passed.

I live in a small hometown called “Bandar Kinrara” which is located at Puchong Selangor. I grew up there and I lived with my parents and my 3 siblings. I have 1 sister and 2 brothers which makes me the middle child.

Throughout my 20 years I have never really experienced moving to places because I have always stayed in Puchong, Selangor in a small town called “Bandar Kinrara”. I was born and raised in Puchong, I even went to schools there and met my best friends there. I love my hometown because all the necessities can be acquired here such as mosques, supermarkets, a variety of restaurants, playgrounds and many more. Although, after I finished my high school years, I had the chance to stay in Kedah to pursue my diploma in Library Science at UiTM, Sg. Petani but sadly due to pandemic I only manage to live there for 1 semester only and a quarter of semester 2. I am about to graduate somewhere between July and August.

Secondly, during my pastime I love listening to music. I listen to all genres of music from pop to rock. If the lyrics and melody are good then I liked it. My biggest passion in life is to help others. During high school, my ambition was to be a therapist or counsellor because I wanted to help those who are struggling with their traumatic past, give advice that could save their life, and made them understand themselves better as well as spreading awareness regarding mental illness. Although, being a therapist and studying about psychology was my passion but I am glad that I decided to continue this course as I have exposed to many new knowledge and skills such as how to look for authentic information, using OPAC, Adobe software and last but not least learn how to do coding from scratch.
