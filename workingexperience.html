WORKING EXPERIENCE

As soon as I graduated high school, I went for a job hunting. It was much harder than i thought it was. At that moment, I was still not 18 so many workplace refuse to take me then that's where I realize how difficult and stressful it was to look for a job in the real world. However, just when I was about to give up, I manage to found a job as a store assistant at a pet store in IOI mall. Sadly, I quit after 1 week of working there due to personal reasons. So, I took a break from hunting and decided to help my mom with the house chores instead.

After the SPM result, I went on a job hunting again. I went through the same process, rejection after rejection then eventually I got a job at 7 Eleven which is very close to my house. For this reason, sometimes I walk to work and also back home and sometimes my parents will come and take me. It is such an amazing opportunity and experience to work there because I manage to attend a class regarding customer services, working shift at 8am to 3pm and sometimes 3pm to 11pm, learn how to stock up the shelves and organize the product and also handling the POS system.


During the customer class service, I met up with other 7 Eleven workers that come from different stores. The instructor of the class explained to us what are the do’s and don'ts when serving the customer. For example, when customers walk in, we must greet them and smile at them. If there’s an angry customer due to the long queue, we should calm them down by saying “Sir/Miss, we will assist you right away.” Moreover, the instructor told us to form a group and begin working together. My group elected me as their group leader. Overall, not only did I get to learn new knowledge but I also had the chance to meet some new friends. After 3 months of working in 7 Eleven, I quit because I was going to continue my studies. Without a doubt, I met some inspiring people while working at 7 Eleven and I even received some great studying tips from them.
